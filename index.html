Why MetaMusic?

Our goal is to encourage musical innovation in Boston.

Boston has world-class resources in music, tech, business, and many other fields. But people in these fields don't get together often. It's like we're working on islands.

If we can bridge these islands, then we can share ideas and innovate. We can think about new ways to teach music, to make music, to enjoy music.

What we're doing here is showing what's happening on the islands. It's up to you to build the bridges.

Where Do The Events Come From?

The events are manually pulled from this list of calendars.

If you want to add your calendar to the list, email boston.metamusic@gmail.com .

Can I Add My Event?

Your event must meet these requirements:

  1. It's related to music.
  2. It's not a show or performance.
  3. It's open to the public.

You can submit individual events through this form.
