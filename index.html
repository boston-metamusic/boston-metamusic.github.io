Why MetaMusic?

Our goal is to encourage musical innovation in Boston.

Boston has world-class resources in music, tech, business, and many other fields. We want to show what is happening in these fields. We hope that this will encourage more collaboration.

Where Do The Events Come From?

Human curators pull relevant events from this list of sources.

Want to add your calendar to the list? Email boston.metamusic@gmail.com .

We will probably miss some events. We apologize, and hope that you will tell us what we should add. See the next section for more about adding events.

Can I Add My Event?

Your event should meet these requirements:

  1. It's related to music.
  2. It's not a show or performance.
  3. It's open to the public.

You can submit individual events through this form.

Got Ideas? Want to Help?

Great! Email us at boston.metamusic@gmail.com. We would love to hear what you think.
